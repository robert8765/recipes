Chocolate Ganache

Ingredients:
1 part semi-sweet chocolate
1 part heavy cream (a.k.a. double cream)

Directions:
1. Make sure chocolate is in small pieces, break or chop if necessary.
2. Heat cream to just below a simmer.
3. Remove cream from heat.
4. Add chocolate to cream and let sit to soften the chocolate.
5. Once the chocolate seems softened, stir together to form the ganache.
